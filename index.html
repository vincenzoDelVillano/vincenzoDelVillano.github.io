About

I am a self-taught and highly motivated application developer. I leverage my technical background to build applications that focus on mathematical and statistical modeling of physical processes.

I have developed applications for Windows, Android, and iOS. I am fluent in C#, Java, MATLAB, Mathematica, and Visual Basic. I hold certifications and degrees in Physics, Chemical Engineering, and Nuclear Engineering. MBA Candidate.
